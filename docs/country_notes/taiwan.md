### Two different "Taichung" terminals — do not conflate
- **CPC Taichung LNG Terminal** (二接 / "No. 2 LNG Terminal", GEM `T100000130317`, operator CPC Corporation Taiwan). This is the one GIIGNL's "Taichung" / "Taichung Expansion" rows refer to.
- **Taichung (TaiPower) LNG Terminal** (五接 / "No. 5 LNG Terminal", GEM `T100000130892`, sponsor Taiwan Electric Power Co, proposed ~3 MTPA). Separate terminal, different sponsor; not in GIIGNL. The TaiPower Yahoo article (台電擬建第四、五天然氣接收站) is about TaiPower's 4th/5th terminals, NOT CPC's phases.

### CPC Taichung phase trajectory (verified 2026-05-28)
GEM models all phases as units of one terminal; the capacity total walks up as phases commission:
- **Phase 1 + 2 + "Phase 2 expansion"** → 8 MTPA, **operating** (reached 2026). Matches GIIGNL table (6.1 + 1.9) and the p.48 narrative.
- **Phase 3** → +2 to **10 MTPA**, **under construction** (ground broken 2022, Bechtel EPC, 2× 180,000 m³ tanks, ~end-2026 completion).
- **Phase 4** (港外擴建四期) → +3 to **13 MTPA**, **proposed / in EIA** (Daigas awarded FEED, 4× 180,000 m³ tanks, target ~2029).
- These sit inside CPC's NT$350bn natural-gas build-out covering four CPC receiving stations (Yung-An, Taichung, Guantang/Taoyuan No.3, Kaohsiung Intercontinental 洲際). Sources: [OGJ Phase III](https://www.ogj.com/pipelines-transportation/lng/article/14279843/cpc-begins-taichung-phase-iii-lng-terminal-expansion), [2b1st](https://2b1stconsulting.com/cpc-taichung-lng-terminal-phase-3/), Yahoo TW 中油3,500億.
