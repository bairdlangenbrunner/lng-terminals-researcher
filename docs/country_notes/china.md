## Research Tips
- 158 China terminals in the GEM export (Q2 2026) — second-largest country coverage
- Trade press in English often lags Chinese sources by 1-3 days
- For inland regas (e.g. Sichuan, Yunnan), provincial regulators are primary; national sources may miss these
- COSCO Shipping Energy and China Merchants Energy appear as both vessel owners and (occasionally) terminal participants — confirm role per project
