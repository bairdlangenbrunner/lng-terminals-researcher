## Research Tips
- 51 Japan terminals in GEM — most are operating import facilities with stable data
- Japanese regulator filings often have English summaries but deep filings are Japanese-only
- TEPCO, Tokyo Gas, Osaka Gas, Chubu Electric are the primary import sponsors
