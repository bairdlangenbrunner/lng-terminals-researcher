# South Korea

## Resources
- MOTIE English: `motie.go.kr/eng/`
- KOGAS IR: `kogas.or.kr/eng/main.do`
- DART (filings): `dart.fss.or.kr`
- KIND (DART English mirror): `kind.krx.co.kr`
- Seoul Economic Daily English: `en.sedaily.com` (faster DART proxy)

## Research Tips
- DART filings sometimes use regional euphemisms ("Asia-region utility") instead of named counterparty — check trade press for actual names
- KOGAS dominates import terminals; private regas projects (Boryeong, etc.) increasing
- Hyundai Glovis appears as both shipper and occasional terminal participant
